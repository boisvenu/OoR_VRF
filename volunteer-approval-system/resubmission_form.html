Faculty of Medicine & Dentistry

Volunteer Request Form

Before engaging a volunteer, the Principal Investigator (PI) must complete this form to obtain FoMD internal approval. This ensures compliance with institutional policies, risk management, and employment agreements while aligning with FoMD’s strategic goals.

Process Overview:

  • Submission – The PI completes this form with input from their Department HR and ADM.
  • Department Chair Review – The request is sent to the Department Chair for endorsement.
  • Department HR Review – After Chair approval, Department HR reviews the request.
  • Office of Research Review – Final decision is made by an FoMD Vice-Dean of Research (or delegate).
  • Decision Notification – The requestor will be notified by email.
  • ADMs are notified as the request moves through the approval process.

Important Notes:

  • Always refer to the Volunteer Procedure if you are unsure about allowable tasks and duties associated with your volunteer.
  • This form must be completed before recruiting a volunteer.
  • Incomplete or duplicate requests will be rejected.
  • Volunteer registration, waivers, and background checks (if needed) must be obtained through the Risk Management & Insurance Office and retained by your department. These documents are not required for this request.

Questions? Contact FoMD Office of Research (vdradmin@ualberta.ca).

Volunteer Risk Management

Risk Management Disclaimer

In order to manage the risks and liabilities inherent in volunteer activities, university faculties and departments must ensure compliance with university policies and procedures. Failure to meet these requirements may result in delays or denial of the volunteer request. This includes:

  • Registering all volunteers to ensure eligibility for WCB coverage.
  • Obtaining a signed volunteer waiver or informed consent form (for minors) to clarify duties, duration, and reporting relationships.
  • Storing all volunteer waivers in accordance with university guidelines.
  • Providing appropriate training and hazard/safety orientations.
  • Immediately reporting all incidents or injuries associated with volunteer activity.

If not already completed, you will recieve further instructions about obtaining a waiver and background check (if required) upon final approval of this request.

Security/Background Check Requirements

To protect the privacy and identity of volunteers, all volunteer waivers and background checks (if applicable) are managed through Risk Management & Insurance with Finance, Procurement, and Planning. The requirements for this documentation will have been provided to you in consultation with your department HR and ADM prior to submitting this request.
